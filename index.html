01 — The mechanism

It's position, not magic.

Professionally mixed films and songs place the lead voice dead-centre in the stereo field, while music and ambience spread wide. VoiceFront exploits that — no machine learning required.

L
R
VOICE
01

Captures your tab's audio

Chrome's tab capture API streams the live audio — nothing leaves your browser.

02

Runs a live FFT every 512-sample hop

An AudioWorklet breaks the signal into frequency bins, continuously, with no perceptible lag.

03

Checks left vs. right, per bin

Equal in both channels → centre → voice. Different → panned wide → music or ambience.

04

You mix the two back together

Voice and Background become independent, gain-controlled streams — balance them however you like.

03 — The controls

Three sliders. That's the whole interface.

🎙 Voice

Dialogue volume

Boosts the isolated centre-channel speech, independent of everything else playing.

🎵 Background

Music & noise volume

Controls everything panned wide — score, ambience, crowd. Drop to zero for voice-only.

🎯 Sharpness

Separation strength

Higher isolates more tightly, with more artefacts. Tune per track for the cleanest split.

No uploads

Audio is processed entirely inside your browser tab. Nothing is sent anywhere.

No AI models

Pure signal processing — a live FFT and a stereo panning mask. No cloud inference.

No server

There's nothing to be down. It runs, fully, on your machine.

No cost

Free and MIT licensed. Read the source, fork it, build on it.
